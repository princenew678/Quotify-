✦ FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need to create an account to use Quotify?
+
No. Quotify works instantly in your browser — no signup, no email, no password. Just open the app and start creating invoices. If you want to save your data permanently, upgrading to Pro gives you cloud sync.
Can I use Quotify for Indian GST invoices?
+
Yes. You can set the tax rate to 18% (or any rate) for every invoice. The tax amount is calculated automatically and shown separately on your invoice and PDF. Full GST invoice format support is on our roadmap.
Is my invoice data safe and private?
+
On the Free plan, your data lives in your browser session. Nothing is sent to any server. On Pro, data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share, sell, or access your invoice data.
Can I send invoices directly to clients from the app?
+
Currently you can generate a PDF and attach it to your own email. Direct email sending from the app is coming in a future update — it's one of our most-requested features.
What currencies does Quotify support?
+
USD, INR, EUR, GBP, JPY, and AUD. Switching currency updates every number in the app instantly — stats, invoices, totals, everything. More currencies are being added based on user demand.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
+
Absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click — no questions asked, no penalty, no lock-in period. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
